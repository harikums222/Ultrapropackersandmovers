Our Services

Home Relocation Services (Full/Partial)

Safe, door to door house shifting for 1RK to 4BHK in and around Chennai and across Tamil Nadu, with full packing, loading, transport, unloading, and basic setup.

  • Multi layer packing for TV, fridge, washing machine and glass items
  • Cot, sofa, and cupboard dismantling and re fixing where possible
  • Lift/stairs handling with trained helpers, no local outsourcing
  • Written quotation + basic transit insurance options available

Office Shifting

Planned office shifting for small and mid-size offices, minimizing downtime with systematic packing of IT systems, documents, and furniture. We ensure your office is set up on schedule.

  • Weekend/night moves on request to avoid business disruption
  • Labeled carton system for each department/room
  • Safe packing for computers, monitors, printers, furniture & utilities
  • Basic dismantling of workstations, racks & desks
  • Pre-move inventory + handover inspection
  • Basic safety assurance + optional transit insurance

Expert Packing

Professional packing-only service for households and offices if you arrange your own vehicle. We bring materials and trained packers to safely pack everything room-wise and hand over ready-to-load cartons.

  • We supply all materials: bubble wrap, corrugated sheets, heavy-duty cartons & labels
  • Room-wise packing (kitchen, bedroom, living) with clear labeling in Tamil/English
  • Special care for fragile items: TV/monitor screens, glassware, kitchen appliances
  • Furniture wrapping & basic disassembly (cots, tables) if needed
  • Before/after photos + videos shared via WhatsApp for your records
  • Handover inspection & inventory list before we leave

Bike/Scooty Transport

Door-to-door bike and scooty transport starting from Chennai, using full wrapping and secure loading—no ropes or loose tying.

  • Full bubble wrap + stretch film on frame, tank & plastic parts
  • Mirrors, handlebar & indicators padded/protected
  • Pre/post-loading photos & videos via WhatsApp
  • Basic condition note (scratches/dents) signed before loading
  • Secure wheel chocks + harness in dedicated space
  • Transit insurance option available

Four Wheeler Transport

Car transport starting from Chennai via verified professional car carriers. Ultra Pro handles full pickup, coordination, and delivery for hassle-free service.

  • Door pickup from your location to carrier yard/loading
  • Pre-loading inspection + photos (exterior, tyres, dents noted)
  • Open/enclosed carrier options based on car value/distance
  • Single point of contact—updates from booking to delivery
  • Delivery coordination + final handover inspection
  • Transit insurance support available

Custom Solutions

Tailored solutions for non-standard moves, we handle unique jobs like intra-building shifts or single-item transport with the same care as full relocations.

  • Same apartment/floor shifting (no vehicle needed)
  • Single items: fridge, wardrobe, cot, sofa, etc
  • Packed boxes to short-term storage drop-off
  • Minimal luggage for elders/students (light loads)
  • Fragile/specialty: plants, fish tanks, paintings, or musical instruments
  • Quick same-day packing help for partial moves
